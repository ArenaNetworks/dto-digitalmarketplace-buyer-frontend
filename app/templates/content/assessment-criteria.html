Assessment criteria

Criteria for all services

  • Work in a multidisciplinary team in an agile delivery environment.
  • Communicate with a wide range of disciplines both internally and externally.
  • Present findings, insights and recommendations to justify decisions.
  • Establish relationships to support delivery of services.

Criteria for each service

  • User research and design
  • Content and publishing
  • Marketing, communications and community engagement
  • Software engineering and Development
  • Data science
  • Recruitment
  • Cyber security
  • Delivery and governance
  • Operations
  • Change, training and transformation
  • Strategy and policy
  • Digital products
  • Emerging technology prototyping

User research and design

Case studies must demonstrate 3 or more of the following:

  • Plan, design, conduct and analyse user research in an agile delivery environment.
  • Recruit participants from diverse audiences, including people with disability, CALD audiences and other minority groups. Provide facilities for usability testing.
  • Visually communicate user research through presentations, journey maps, videos etc. to clarify key outcomes and generate empathy for end users
  • Map existing user experiences and identify user needs. Find opportunities for reducing failure, reducing the cost of a service and better meet the user need.
  • Develop concepts based on research insights. Iteratively test concepts with end users, from paper sketches to HTML prototypes.
  • Design for different screen sizes and input methods (for example, touch, mouse and keystroke) following style guides and WCAG 2.0 AA guidelines.
  • Develop concepts based on research insights. Iteratively test concepts with end users, from paper sketches to HTML prototypes.
  • Perform accessibility audits to uncover accessibility issues and get recommended fixes.

Content and publishing

Case studies must demonstrate 2 or more of the following:

  • Providing content that meets the needs of the user as clearly, simply and quickly as possible. Formats include plain language copy, photography, illustration, interactive media and video.
  • Creating content for different platforms and devices, following style guides and accessibility and cultural diversity best practice.
  • Experience with content platforms (for example Sitecore, GovCMS), content governance frameworks and detailed editorial calendars.
  • Defining and maintaining taxonomies, tagging systems and metadata.
  • A/B testing to improve and share insights on content performance.

Marketing, communications and community engagement

Case studies must demonstrate 1 or more of the following:

  • Creating targeted digital marketing campaigns that have delivered user engagement and significant product or service usage.
  • Experience in quantifying marketing impact and SEO performance and strong understanding of technical SEO - sitemaps, crawl budget, canonicalization, etc.
  • Develop press strategies and provide communications guidance.
  • Create strategies to increase engagement within communities.
  • Plan, produce, and execute events.
  • Experience with public sector account and relationship management

Software engineering and development

Case studies must demonstrate 3 or more of the following:

  • Research and weigh up competing technology choices and make informed decisions based on user needs, with a preference for open source technology.
  • Experience using and deploying on cloud-based platforms.
  • Use and implementation of modern front end web programming techniques, such as HTML5, CSS3, AJAX, REST and JSON.
  • Good development skills in one or more of Ruby, Python, JavaScript, with familiarity with one or more open source web frameworks such as Rails, Django.
  • Proven capability in managing technology implementation projects Track record of successfully taking an evolutionary architecture approach to software architecture.
  • Experience building and scaling high-traffic websites and/or high volume transaction processing and analysis platforms.
  • Experience designing and implementing scalable and robust approaches to caching, security and databases (including relational, for example, MySQL and PostgreSQL; and NoSQL, for example, Cassandra and MongoDB).
  • Deep and wide experience with infrastructure and platform technologies like AWS, Google Cloud, Cloud Foundry, Deis, or Tsuru.
  • Deep and wide knowledge of the plumbing of the internet (TCP/IP, routing, bridging, HTTP, SSL, DNS, mail).
  • Experience designing and implementing scalable and robust approaches to caching, security and databases (including relational, for example, MySQL and PostgreSQL; and NoSQL, for example, Cassandra and MongoDB).
  • Experience with automated configuration management, deployment and testing solutions.
  • Experience configuring and managing Linux servers for serving a dynamic website.
  • Experience debugging a complex multi-server service. Familiarity with configuration management tools like Puppet and Chef.

Data science

Data science case studies must demonstrate:

  • Solving difficult, non-routine analysis problems, working with large, complex data sets.
  • Experience with statistical methods such as parametric and nonparametric significance testing.
  • Experience with statistical software (e.g., R, Julia, MATLAB, pandas) and database languages (e.g. SQL).
  • Providing insight or recommendations (e.g. cost-benefit, forecasting, experiment analysis) through visual displays of quantitative information.

Recruitment

Recruitment case studies must demonstrate:

  • Assessing applications against specified employment criteria
  • Create shortlists of preferred candidates
  • Deliver assessment tools and activities, such as scripting behavioural interview questions and conducting interviews.
  • Coordinate pre-employment screening, where required

Cyber security

Case studies must demonstrate 1 or more of the following:

  • Research and development to better detect, deter and respond to emerging cyber security issues.
  • Discover, communicate and explain security vulnerabilities to development teams to ensure secure coding In a multi-product, continuous delivery environment.
  • Automated testing to align with continuous integration.
  • Carry out assessments (penetration testing, Web Application security testing, vulnerability scanning, threat modelling, etc).
  • Experience with Security testing tools, (eg Nessus, RKHunter, Fail2Ban, BURP, Cucumber, Netsparker).
  • Recommend improvements to fix vulnerabilities in products, infrastructures, and processes.
  • Security architecture reviews, IRAP assessments, Risk assessments, and writing of security related documentation.
  • Experience with Data Loss Prevention and Data Protection. Security incident or emergency response.
  • Monitoring of IT equipment, facility access or infrastructure access.
  • Recovery of information from computers and storage devices.

Change, training and transformation

Case studies must demonstrate 2 or more of the following:

  • Work at the team, portfolio or program level, to help organisations establish the right processes for managing a portfolio of work in an agile way.
  • Experience delivering change activities, leading stakeholder engagement, identifying learning and development opportunities, and driving organisation-wide cultural change.
  • Automated testing to align with continuous integration.
  • Carry out assessments (penetration testing, Web Application security testing, vulnerability scanning, threat modelling, etc).
  • Assess business readiness, design and implement target operating models, performance management and organisational learning.
  • Design and delivery of training programs.
