About the Marketplace

We're transforming how government buys digital and technology services.

Our purpose

The Marketplace is an initiative under the National Innovation and Science Agenda. Our purpose is to make it easier for smaller businesses to compete for the government’s $5 billion a year spend on information and communications technology (ICT) products and services.

Based on a successful UK model, the Marketplace is an online directory of digital and technological services for government agencies to procure ICT solutions from businesses of all sizes. It’s also a place for government buyers to discover new ideas and challenge ICT suppliers to develop more innovative government services.
