Delivery Manager

The delivery manager supports the team by identifying and removing the obstacles stopping the team from delivering. Also known as the scrum master, they facilitate the daily stand-up and weekly team meetings, and make sure the backlog and team spaces are up to date.

Developer

Developers build software in a team with a relentless focus on how it will be used. They write quality, well-tested code and try to find the simplest solution to a given problem. Most will specialise in being a frontend or a backend developer: a good team has a mix of both. As they work, they are expected to keep security, accessibility and open standards in mind, and improve (‘refactor’) the technical implementation as they go along.

Day rates

Junior $500 - $2000
Senior $1100 - $2000 $1600

In a multi-product, continuous delivery environment, they must meet the challenge of providing the most effective vulnerability information across teams.

Performance and Web Analyst

Digital performance analysts sit at the heart of a team, working to specify, collect and present the key performance data and analysis for their service. They are essential to how the government continuously measures, assesses, and improves performance in transacting with the public. Analysts support service managers by generating new and useful information and translating it into actions that allow them to iteratively improve their service for users.

Product Manager

The owner of the product backlog, the product manager, is responsible for prioritising user stories, attending daily stand-ups and accepting stories when they are delivered. They are on hand to answer product questions from the delivery team and work closely with the user researchers to make sure the product is meeting user needs.

Service Designer

The service designer works as part of a multi-disciplined team to design user-focused and successful services. Service designers deliver designs that meet web standards, ensuring key elements are built-in from the outset. They contribute to the development and continual enhancement of products, partnering with colleagues to facilitate a consistent user experience.

Day rates

Range (low-high) Median

Senior $1100 - $2000 $1600

Technical Lead / Technical Architect

The technical lead is the most senior technical person in the team. Their role is to break down complex problems and lead by example in writing quality code. They engage with non-technical people at all levels of seniority, ensure the product is technically aligned with other areas of the agency, and provide coaching and feedback to other technical members of the team.

Technical architects have an equal balance of technical expertise gained through current, hands-on involvement in developing real-world systems, and excellent interpersonal skills allied with the ability to quickly develop strong working relationships in high-pressure environments. They will ideally have experience in development of transformational digital services on existing projects and/or rapid development and scaling of high-volume ‘green field’ services.

User Researcher

Working closely with the product manager and the designers in the team, the user researcher is responsible for building understanding of the service's users and their behaviour, and providing insight to the team about how users interact with the service. They take the lead on running fortnightly research sessions with real users, and communicating findings back to the whole team.

Web Operations Engineer (Web Devops)

Responsible for keeping the service online, web operations engineers are critical to any digital service. They work closely with developers to make sure that all technology is built with consideration to how it will be operated, and put the foundations in place for the service to be hosted and deployed to preview and live environments. Along with other developers in the team, they are expected to share the out-of-hours support responsibility for the service.

About the rates and definitions

The day rates shown are given as a guide only. Each individual seller has their own rates on their seller page which can be accessed through the Marketplace catalogue.

Senior roles will need to have a minimum of 5 years experience and are expected to:

  • build and manage relations with board level stakeholders
  • coach, develop and lead others within the delivery team and stakeholders supporting the team
  • have considerable, demonstrable experience in agile development and leading teams within structured programme and project environments
  • have experience in delivering a range of transactional services through digital/web channels.
